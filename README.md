<h1 align="center">📊 Demand Forecasting &amp; Sales Performance Insights</h1>

<p align="center">
  This project analyzes historical sales data to uncover <b>demand trends</b>, <b>revenue performance</b>, and <b>product insights</b>.
  It helps identify key drivers of growth and supports <b>better forecasting and business decisions</b>.
</p>

<h2>📌 Overview</h2>
<p>
  An interactive Tableau dashboard built to explore sales performance, demand patterns, and revenue behaviour across products and regions.
</p>

<h2>⚙️ Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>📈 Demand and Revenue trend analysis</li>
  <li>🏆 Top-performing products and states</li>
  <li>🔍 Demand vs Revenue segmentation</li>
  <li>📊 Interactive Tableau dashboard for business insights</li>
</ul>

<h2>💡 Key Insights</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Demand and revenue show a <b>consistent upward trend</b></li>
  <li>A small group of products contributes most of the revenue</li>
  <li>Different demand-revenue clusters suggest <b>pricing and product opportunities</b></li>
  <li>Low-performing items highlight areas for <b>optimization</b></li>
</ul>
